One day, Lily and Hope were playing in the forest when they came across a group of bullies. The bullies started to pick on Lily, but Hope stood up to them. She barked and growled at the bullies, and they eventually ran away. Lily was so grateful to Hope for protecting her, and she knew that she would always have a friend in her.
